How It Works

From symptom to solution in four steps

Our streamlined process ensures patients get the right care at the right time — without unnecessary friction.

Describe

Patients share symptoms through guided intake forms enhanced by Thought Seeing AI for thorough, accurate capture.

Match

Our algorithm identifies the best-fit specialist based on condition, availability, patient history, and preferences.

Consult

Live video visit with real-time AI assistance — the clinician sees diagnostic suggestions, relevant research, and risk factors as they consult.

Follow-up

Automated care plans, prescription delivery, lab orders, and check-in reminders keep patients on the path to recovery.

About

Built on the legacy of healing

Named after the ancient Greek sanctuary of Epidaurus — a place where the sick travelled in search of divine healing — Epidaurus Telemedical carries that mission into the digital age.

We believe that geography should never determine the quality of healthcare someone receives. Our Thought Seeing platform combines decades of clinical research with cutting-edge artificial intelligence to deliver diagnostic support that augments — never replaces — the human clinician.

Founded by a multidisciplinary team of physicians, engineers, and healthcare operators, we are committed to building technology that serves patients first.
