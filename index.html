Thought Seeing™ · Perception Engine

Thought Seeing

From surgical neuroscience to entertainment. A perception engine that lets you see what others think — and share what you see.

Thought Seeing began as a surgical adjuvant — technology designed to predict perioperative comatose risk in patients with brain injury or disease. We have now adapted this into a non-invasive tool that predicts human perceptions in learning and recall, for entertainment purposes.

How It Works

Three steps to shared perception

A simple, intuitive experience — from headset to thought stream.

Put on the headset

Calibrated to your circadian rhythm, the headset creates an immersive environment tuned to your biology and attention patterns.

Think freely

The engine reads perception patterns, not brain waves. Your visual thoughts, memories, and ideas are captured non-invasively.

Share or receive

Visual thoughts stream between connected users — for entertainment, learning, or cross-cultural communication.

About

From the operating room to the living room

Epidaurus Telemedical builds perception technology at the intersection of neuroscience, artificial intelligence, and human connection.

Our Thought Seeing platform was originally developed as a perioperative neurosurgical adjuvant — predicting comatose risk in patients undergoing brain surgery. Today, we've adapted that same precision into a non-invasive experience for entertainment, education, and empathic communication.

We believe the future of human interaction isn't faster typing or better screens — it's shared perception. Thought Seeing makes that future tangible.
